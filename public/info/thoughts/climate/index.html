humanism is not at odds with technology #

Like many, I often find myself associating modern technology with negative practices, be it social media, invasive advertising, electronic waste, privacy concerns - I could go on.

It is easy to get caught up in these systems that are eroding our way of life, but I want to focus on the positive impacts of technology for a bit.

  • OpenStreetMap
    • Used for a variety of things, one of the more noble uses of this technology is using OSM data to assist in humanitarian aid. A good example is how it was used (in conjunction with Crisis Commons volunteers) to provide satellite imagery to Haiti after their 2010 earthquake.
